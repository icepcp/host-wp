Frequently Asked Questions

Why are the prices so low? What's the catch?

As the company is fairly new, we do not have much operational costs as of now. We are aiming to get more clients and lower the prices of hosting in general.
We currently also do not have a control panel to manage your subscription, but we are actively working on it.

How do I get a plan?

Head to the Pricing page and click Order now for whichever plan you'd like. We will process the order from our side (typically takes 12 hours or less) and you will get an email once your instance is ready

What are the limits on plans with email?

You are limited to 200 sends per day. If you need to send more emails daily, please contact us and we will figure something out

How does storage work?

Storage is pooled among your instances, so you are able to purchase 10GB of storage and use it across your email and website.
All instances come with 1GB storage included to get you started. 1GB is enough for almost everyone, as a typical WordPress install is roughly 60MB, however, if it is not enough for you, you can purchase more.
